About DragonShield Security

Elite offensive security professionals dedicated to protecting your digital realm.

Our Story

DragonShield Security was founded in 2014 by a team of offensive security veterans who shared a common frustration: too many organizations were receiving penetration testing reports filled with automated scanner output and generic recommendations that did nothing to actually improve their security posture.

We built DragonShield on a simple principle — every engagement should deliver genuine, actionable value. Our testers don't just run tools and compile output. They think like adversaries, manually probing and testing your defenses with the same creativity and persistence that real attackers employ.

Over the past twelve years, we've grown from a three-person consultancy to a team of over 40 security professionals serving clients across financial services, healthcare, technology, manufacturing, government, and critical infrastructure sectors. Our growth has been driven entirely by the quality of our work and the strength of our client relationships — because in this industry, reputation is everything.

The dragon in our name and identity represents what we strive to be for every client: a powerful, vigilant guardian standing watch over your most valuable digital assets. The starry portal in our visual identity represents the threshold between security and vulnerability — the boundary we help our clients defend every day.

Our Mission

To strengthen the security posture of organizations worldwide by delivering penetration testing and offensive security services of uncompromising quality, providing the actionable intelligence our clients need to stay ahead of evolving threats.

Our Vision

A world where every organization has the insight and capability to defend itself against sophisticated cyber threats — where security is a strategic advantage, not just a compliance checkbox.
