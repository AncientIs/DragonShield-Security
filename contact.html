Vulnerability scanning is an automated process that identifies known vulnerabilities based on signatures and configuration checks. Penetration testing goes significantly further — our experts manually exploit vulnerabilities to demonstrate real-world impact, chain multiple findings together, identify business logic flaws, and test defenses that automated tools cannot evaluate. Penetration testing provides a realistic assessment of your actual risk, not just a list of potential issues.
